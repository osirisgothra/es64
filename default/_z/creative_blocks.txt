In creative mode, open chat (t) and type:
/give <playername> <BlockID> 1 <Metadata>

Example:
/give Shoeboxam 5 1 4
Crates appear in Shoeboxam's inventory.


-MCPatcher or Optifine required
-MCPatcher required to see blocks rendered correctly in inventory
-Soartex is required to see these blocks
